Biography

I am Felix Mwangi a wildlife and landscape photographer based in Nairobi, Kenya.
Over the last five years, I have had the pleasure of documenting some of the most iconic lanndscapes in Africa and captured over a thousand species of different animals.
I have been named as one of the top wildlife photographers in Africa and featured in numerous publications.
